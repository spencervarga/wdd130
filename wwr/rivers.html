Rafting Adventures of the Month

Salmon River

Experience the thrill of conquering the mighty Salmon River! Known as the "River of No Return," this adventure will take you through stunning landscapes, challenging rapids, and pristine wilderness. Navigate through exciting Class III and IV rapids, surrounded by rugged canyons and abundant wildlife. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, the Salmon River offers an unforgettable white-water experience for all adventurers.

Little Colorado River

Embark on a breathtaking journey down the Little Colorado River! Discover the beauty of this hidden gem as you navigate through crystal-clear waters and colorful canyons. The Little Colorado River offers a mix of calm stretches and thrilling Class II rapids, making it an ideal adventure for both beginners and experienced rafters. Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the landscape, surrounded by towering cliffs and the unique geology of the region. Join us for an unforgettable rafting experience on the Little Colorado River!
